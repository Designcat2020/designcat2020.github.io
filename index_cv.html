SUN, YAN

As a practicing architect, she has been delicate in the architecture field for decades. She has the capability to manage all phases of projects from beginning to end, design, direct, and supervise all stages include in many large-sized building projects. And cooperate with the group members to complete quality buildings. Act as the primary point of contact with clients, develop design strategies, lead technical processes and execute projects successfully.

As a research scholar, she uses data visualization, mapping, and program technology for spatial research. She studies different residential models and care theories to re-thinking architecture practics. Meanwhile, as a guest expert, she leads lectures on Narrative Cartography in the School of Journalism & Communication at Nanjing University.
